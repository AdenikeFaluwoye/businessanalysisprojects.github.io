Optimizing Transaction Tracking and Reporting for Small Businesses

Cross-functional initiative to improve small business financial management through transaction tracking, invoicing, and reporting dashboards.

Project Overview

This initiative was aimed at helping small business owners track sales, expenses, staff salaries, generate invoices, and visualize performance through a mobile application.

Business Problem

Small business owners often face errors and inefficiencies due to manual bookkeeping or scattered tools, resulting in poor visibility of transactions and business performance.

Solution

Mobile application to record transactions, generate invoices, maintain customer records, and provide dashboards. Offline functionality ensures uninterrupted use.

Key Features

  • Transaction recording: sales, expenses, credit
  • Invoice generation for customers
  • Analytical dashboards with reports
  • Customer management with notes
  • User authentication via OTP
  • Offline operation with auto-sync
