Small Business Financial Management Mobile Application

This cross-functional product development initiative delivered a mobile-based financial management SaaS solution enabling small businesses to manage sales, expenses, invoicing, and reporting in real time.

Project Overview

Designed to help small business owners and entrepreneurs efficiently track sales, expenses, staff salaries, and generate invoices through a mobile application.

Business Problem

Small business owners often rely on manual bookkeeping or scattered tools, leading to errors, inefficiencies, and limited reporting capabilities.

Solution

A mobile application that records transactions, generates invoices, maintains customer records, and provides analytical dashboards. Works offline and syncs automatically when an internet connection is available.

Key Features

  • Record sales, expenses, and credit transactions
  • Generate invoices for customers
  • User authentication via OTP
  • Analytical dashboard with sales and expense reports
  • Customer management and note-taking
  • Offline functionality with automatic sync
